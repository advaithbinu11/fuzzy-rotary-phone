How to Make a Paper Airplane

Paper airplanes are a easy and great way to get started on origami! Here is some instructions on how you can make your very own paper airplanes.

  • Fold a piece of letter-sized paper in half lengthwise then unfold it.
  • Fold the corners to the center fold on the paper to make two triangles.
  • Fold the triangles again to the center fold. This makes the paper look like a triangle.
  • Fold the paper along the center fold you did in step 1.
  • Finally, fold the angled side of the airplane halfways towrds the center fold.
  • Flip the airplane and repeat.
  • Now, pick up your airplane and enjoy!

How to Make a Ninja Star

Ninja Stars are a bit more harder than a paper airplane but they still are easy for anyone to learn! Here is some instructions on how you can make your very own ninja star.

  • Fold two piece of letter-sized paper in half lengthwise. Then unfold them both.
  • Fold the top and bottom sides to the center fold. Then fold the top fold on top of the bottom one.
  • For one paper, fold the top left corner in and down. Fold the bottom right corner in and up.
  • For the other paper, fold the top left corner in and up. Fold the bottom right corner in and down.
  • Fold the top right corner down and the bottom left corner up on the first paper.
  • Fold the top right corner up and the bottom left corner down on the second paper.

Your paper should look like the picture in the top right.

  • Flip the second one over to the other side and rotate as shown in the bottom left photo.
  • Place the first one on top of the second one.
  • Fold the triangle on the right side up diagonally to the left. Slot it into the left flap. Repeat on other side and flip the star.
  • Repeat the previous step with the other side.
  • You're done! Enjoy your amazing ninja star!
